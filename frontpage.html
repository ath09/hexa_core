About us

VJTI college provides hostel facilities for B.Tech , M.tech and Diploma students The college hostel is fully furnished with all the modern facilities and has an intake of 554 students locate at the Southern end of the campus comprises of 4 blocks named A, B, C and D. The admission in the college hostel is fully based on the merit-cum-need basis and is available only for UG and Diploma students. Preference is given to students residing outside the city of Mumbai. Hostel facility is not available for direct second year diploma as well as post graduate students. There are four messes run by students. Hostel fees are based on three seaters, two seaters or single seater including electricity charges. The hostel students' amenities fund and medical charges are payable at the begining of each term.
